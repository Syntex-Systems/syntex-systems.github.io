Experimental Roots

Innovation Archive

Projects that paved the way. These experiments are currently discontinued as we focus our resources on Codebrae, but their DNA lives on in our technology.

Discontinued

OSIO

A web-based operating system simulation. OSIO pushed the boundaries of what browsers could do, offering a desktop environment with multitasking and custom scripting capabilities within a single tab.

Discontinued

Synchrez

A precise media synchronization protocol. It allowed multiple devices to play music in perfect unison over local networks, exploring low-latency web socket communications.

Discontinued

Grabits

A frictionless local file transfer tool. Grabits eliminated the need for cloud uploads when moving files between devices on the same Wi-Fi, focusing on speed and privacy.
